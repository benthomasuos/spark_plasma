FCT HPD 25 - Quick Start Guide

Machine startup

  1. Turn on machine
  2. Turn on emergency waterCheck pressure is ~ 4 bar
  3. Turn on building compressed air supplyCheck pressure is ~ 6 bar
  4. Turn on extraction fan
  5. Log in to SPS machine
  6. Check and acknowledge all alarmsReport any alarms that cannot be acknowledged

Sample preparation

Item checklist

  • Top support
  • Top ram
  • Mould
  • Bottom ram
  • Bottom support
  • Graphite foils
  • Graphite felt jacket
  • Wear pads (OPTIONAL)

Procedure

  1. Measure out sample powder
  2. Cut out graphite foil discs
  3. Cut out graphite foil liner
  4. Assemble liner, bottom ram and lower foil discs
  5. Load sample powder
  6. Assemble upper foil discs and top ram
  7. Pre-press the powder inside the tools using the desktop hydraulic press
  8. Unload the tooling from the press
  9. Wrap the graphite felt coating around the mould
